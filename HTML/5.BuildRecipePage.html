Delicious Chicken Curry

This is a simple and flavorful chicken curry recipe perfect for beginners and quick meals.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Spices (turmeric, chili powder, garam masala)

Instructions

  1. Cut and clean the chicken pieces.
  2. Fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, then cook until soft.
  4. Add chicken and cook until fully done.
